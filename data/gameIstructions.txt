You will stop at the Butcher Shop, Farmers Market, Bakery, and Liquor Store to choose the main dishes for dinner.
The store owners will give you three ingredients to complete the recipe. The goal is to remember all the ingredients.
In the General Store, you will go through the aisles and finish buying all the necessary ingredients.
Finally, you will arrive home and wait for your family to rate the dishes you have prepared.

Game points-
There are four main dishes; each dish has three ingredients.
Each ingredient is worth 5 points. At the end of the game, you'll be graded based on how many points you have.
Max points are 60!

Extra points-
Your family will give you extra points for their favorite dishes.
You can get up to 5 points for every dish. Max points are 20
